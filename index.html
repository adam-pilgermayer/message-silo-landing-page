Message Silo

Clean up the mess of messages

A tool to fix or enrich messages with the power of AI, and make integration simpler for event-driven systems

Get Started

Created for developers who need to handle special cases of dead-lettered messages in different message-brokers

Simple

You can control your message processing with a unified configuration.

Developer Friendly

Full control by our CLI called siloctl. Manage your connections in YAML files.

Free

Run inside your environment with Docker. Try it now!
